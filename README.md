# Global Data Jobs Demand Analysis
![Dashboard Images](https://github.com/abrash073/Global-Data-Jobs-Demand-Analysis/blob/main/Screenshot%202026-06-23%20235556.png?raw=true)

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes the global demand for data-related careers using job market data from various platforms and locations worldwide. The dashboard provides insights into hiring trends, salary benchmarks, job distribution, remote work opportunities, and the most in-demand roles within the data industry.

The goal is to help aspiring and experienced data professionals understand the current job market and make informed career decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze global demand for data-related jobs.
* Identify the highest-paying data roles.
* Discover hiring trends throughout 2024.
* Compare hourly and yearly salaries across job titles.
* Explore remote work opportunities and degree requirements.
* Understand which platforms generate the most job postings.
* Visualize the worldwide distribution of data jobs.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### Main Dashboard

#### Key Metrics

* **Total Jobs Analyzed:** 479K
* **Average Hourly Salary:** $47.62
* **Average Yearly Salary:** $120.59K
* **Salary Rating Score:** 3.5/5

#### Insights

* Data Engineer is the most in-demand role.
* Data Analyst and Data Scientist remain highly sought-after positions.
* Job demand fluctuated throughout 2024, peaking above 55K postings and declining toward year-end.
* Senior-level data roles command significantly higher salaries.

#### Visualizations

* Job demand trends throughout 2024
* Top data jobs by job count
* Hourly vs yearly salary comparison
* Job counts and salary breakdown by role

---

### Drill-Through Analysis (Data Scientist Example)

#### Salary Insights

* Median Yearly Salary: **$125K**
* Median Hourly Salary: **$43.03**

#### Work Environment

* **14%** of Data Scientist roles offer work-from-home opportunities.
* Most job postings still require traditional work arrangements.

#### Education Requirements

* Only a small percentage of jobs explicitly mention degree requirements.
* Skills and experience often play a significant role in hiring decisions.

#### Additional Insights

* Global distribution of Data Scientist opportunities
* Most active hiring platforms
* Employment type distribution (Full-time dominates with 91%)

## 🔍 Key Findings

1. **Data Engineer** is currently the most demanded data role globally.
2. **Machine Learning Engineers** and **Senior Data Scientists** earn some of the highest salaries.
3. Full-time positions account for the vast majority of data job postings.
4. Remote opportunities exist but represent a smaller share of the market.
5. Hiring demand is distributed globally, with strong concentrations in North America and Europe.
6. LinkedIn and other major job platforms contribute the highest number of job listings.
